Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

No Data Collection

Obscura is designed as a completely offline password manager. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Store or transmit your passwords
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking cookies
  • Connect to any external servers

Local Storage Only

All your data, including:

  • Passwords
  • Account information
  • Settings
  • Encrypted vault files

Is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: privacy@obscura.app
